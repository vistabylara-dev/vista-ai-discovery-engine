# Vista AI Discovery Engine

## Mission

Vista AI Discovery Engine helps businesses discover organizations that are publicly showing buying signals for digital services.

The platform continuously analyzes publicly available information, evaluates business opportunities, and ranks companies by commercial intent.

---

# Core Principles

- Never use private data.
- Never access personal information.
- Only analyze publicly available information or information available through permitted APIs.
- Every recommendation must be explainable.
- Every score must include evidence.

---

# Main Components

1. Worker Engine
2. Data Collectors
3. Opportunity Scoring
4. Company Database
5. Dashboard
6. Reports
7. API
8. Authentication

---

# Worker Responsibilities

Workers perform only one task.

Examples:

- Technology Detection
- Website Audit
- SEO Audit
- AI Visibility Audit
- Public Hiring Detection
- News Detection
- Expansion Detection
- Funding Detection
- Performance Analysis

---

# Scoring

Every company receives:

- Opportunity Score
- AI Visibility Score
- Website Quality Score
- Technical Score
- Trust Score

Every score must be explainable.

---

# Goal

Help agencies, consultants, and businesses identify organizations that are likely to benefit from digital services based on publicly available signals.
